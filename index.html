What is Rotaract?

Rotaract originally began in 1968 as a Rotary International youth program in Charlotte, North Carolina, and has grown into a major Rotary-sponsored organization of nearly 10,000 clubs around the world, including over 200,000 members. We are a service, leadership and community service organization for young men and women between the ages 18–30. Clubs around the world also take part in international service projects, in a global effort to bring peace and international understanding to the world.

Rotaract = Rotary in Action

As our motto is “Service Above Self,” Rotaract strives to encourage young professionals to address the physical and social needs of their communities and to promote better relations between all people worldwide through a framework of friendship and service.
