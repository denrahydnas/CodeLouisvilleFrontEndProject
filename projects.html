Rotaract

Projects

Rotaract members identify needs in our community and work to fill them, by proposing, planning, and carrying out ideas for service projects both here in the Ohio Valley and around the world. From planting trees to increase Louisville's tree canopy with Love Louisville Trees to helping local Girl Scout troops stock cookies, we regularly participate in a wide variety of service projects.
