Service Projects

Rotaract members identify needs in our community and work to fill them, by proposing, planning, and carrying out ideas for service projects both here in the Ohio Valley and around the world. From sorting food, to planting trees to increase Louisville's tree canopy with Love Louisville Trees and sorting textbooks to send to South Africa, we regularly participate in a wide variety of service projects.
